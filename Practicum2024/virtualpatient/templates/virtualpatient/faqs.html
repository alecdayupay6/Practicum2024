FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Account Creation/Login
  • How do I reset my password if I forget it?

    Currently, there is no way to reset your password without knowing the old password.

  • What is the difference between a teacher and student?

    Teachers can generate new patients while students cannot.

Patient Generation/Selection
  • How can I edit my generated patients?

    Currently, there is no way to edit any generated patients.

Patient Simulation
  • How can I save my conversation with a patient?

    The only way to save the conversation is to successfully diagnose the patient. If you leave the simulation during a conversation, progress will be lost.

  • Can I reset the simulation?

    After diagnosing the patient, your conversation is saved and cannot be reset. If you leave the simulation during a conversation, your progress will be reset once you simulate the same patient.

Miscellaneous
  • How can I change my password?

    Click your username on the top-right of the page to enter your Profile Settings. There will be an option to change your password. You must know your old password.
